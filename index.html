The project

The present website is intended as a digital version of the catalogue of the Museo della Certosa di Pavia. This is a small museum that hosts a varied collection of paintings, sculptures, drawings, photographs, all propriety or related to the complex of the charterhouse. The Certosa (i.e. chartherhouse) is a rich architectural and artistic complex on the outskirts of the city of Pavia in Lombardy, Italy. To explore further the history of the museum and of the charterhouse, go to "About".

It was our intention to be faithful to the print edition of the Catalogue, which exists only in Italian. All the translation are thus responsability of the author. It was decided also to follow the interior partition of the Catalogue: clicking on "Collection" the landing page presents the six categories in which the objects are divided in the original catalogue. Each section has its own number of catalogue. For each artwork, information is presented about the author, date, medium and subject; in addition, links to relevant related projects or items are provided. It is possible to bulk download the data in the website by going to the page "Download"

Disclaimer

This project is intended only as an exercise in digital collection designing for the exam of Digital Humanities at the University of Pavia. It is thus only achieved partially, as the digitalisation of the entire catalogue would have consituted a much more time-consuming (and I dare say, possible only with a grant) project. Therefore, there are only a few selected items for each section of the catalogue; it was decided to focus on the Paintings section, as it was the more complete on print. All incorrect information in the data is to be attributed to my inexperience in working with this type of artwork.
